Fiber-Rich Carbohydrates

When used properly, there is no need to feel guilty about enjoying carbohydrates (carbs). When eating carbs, focus on naturally grown carbs rather than processed foods. Some of the foods below may need to be limited by some people based on health conditions such as diabetes or obesity. Overall, carbs are safe when enjoyed properly.
Starchy vegetables should be enjoyed only every other day or so, and should not make up the majority of a dinner plate. For vegetables that should be consumed everyday and in large amounts, view the non-starchy vegetables page. Other fiber-rich carbs are fruits and legumes, as well as grains. Fruits are the healhiest forms of fiber and can make a delicious snack.
Examples of heart healthy carbs are listed below.
