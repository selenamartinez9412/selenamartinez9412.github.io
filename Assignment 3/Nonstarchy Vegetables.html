Heart Healthy Veggies

Below is a rainbow list of vegetables that support optimal brain and heart health. When struggling to eat vegetables, eating with your eyes is the key. Make your dinner look delicious with a variety of colors on each dish.
The vegetables listed below are non-starchy and filled with nutrition to maintain healthy mind and heart functionality. Non-starchy Vegetables are filled with essential vitamins and are high in fiber and minerals.
High starch foods are not necessarily unhealthy, but excessive amounts of high starch foods on a daily basis are linked to a higher risk of diabetes, heart diseases, and weight gain. Examples of starchy vegetables to limit are also included below.
