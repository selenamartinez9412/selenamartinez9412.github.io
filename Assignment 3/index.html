Heart and Brain Healthy Habits
Creating a heart and brain healthy lifestyle is a gradual process. Nutritionists suggest that a healhy heart and brain is most accomplishable through a nutritious diet. Certain foods offer vitamins and minerals that support brain health, while others offer no nutritional value. For this reason, incorporating lots of fruits, vegetables, legumes and whole grains can increase your changes of a longer and healthier life.
We can all benefit from making an effort to incorporate these foods into our daily diet. Change does not happen overnight, rather it is a gradual process. While learning about brain and heart healthy foods, try finding a few ways you can incorporate these foods into your diet. Ask yourself where you can swap an unhealthy option for a healthier choice. Most importantly, find what works best for you. Making a healthier choice does not mean omitting your favorite meals, it only asks you to find a healthier way to assemble those recipes.
