High Energy Dog
High Energy dogs are a great companion for those looking for an active friend. High energy dogs enjoy long walks, runs, frisbee, fetch and other outdoor fun. These dogs are great motivators for those looking to spend more time outside. However, these dogs require plenty of activity time and can become unhappy or destructive when left alone inside for long hours.
Who are high energy dogs a great match for?
  • Families with active members.
  • Active individuals looking for an outdoor companion.
  • People with acces to large spaces.
Examples of High Energy Dogs
  • Siberian Husky A furry breed ideal for colder temperatures and an active lifestyle.
  • Golden Retriever A people pleasing lovable friend. These dogs are always happy to be included in an outdoor activity.
  • Border Collie A medium-large dog, bred for hearding cattle. Its origin makes the breed a high energy runner.
  • German Shepherds Also bred for hearding livestock. These dogs love fetching and running.
  • Labrador Retreivers Much like its name indicates, these dogs were created for training and retreiving. These dogs are highly active and easy to train.


Who should avoid high energy dogs?
  • People living in tiny spaces such as apartments or tiny homes.
  • Low energy individuals, or people with physical health conditions.
  • Households who are gone for longs hours at a time.
