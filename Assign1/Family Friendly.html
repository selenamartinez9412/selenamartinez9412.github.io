Family Friendly Dog
Family oriented dogs are a gentle companion for children. These dogs are known for being patient, cuddly and sometimes active.These dogs can range from being a great activity partners for children, and as well as a cuddly homebody! Most importantly, these dogs rarely display aggressive behaviors towards their owners, making them trustworthy around children.
Who are family oriented dogs a great match for?
(The following may range between dog breeds, so make sure to do further research once interested in a specific breed).
Examples of Family Friendly Dog Breeds
  • People living in tiny spaces such as apartments or tiny homes.
  • Low energy individuals, or people with physical health conditions (depending on the breed).
  • For most of these breeds, households who are gone for longs hours at a time are not a good match.
    •

Examples of family friendly dog breeds:
  • Newfoundlands Also known as "Nature's Babysitter", are gentle and patient dogs.
  • Golden Retriever A high activity dog, known for being loyal and kind creatures.
  • Poodles Known for being very kind and gentle. A great match for a calm family with older members.
  • Irish Setter An energetic and playful breed. Great match for children who need someone to play with.
  • Bull Terriers A compact sized dog bred for companionship.
  • BeagleAside from being easy to train, this dog is also a great family friend.
